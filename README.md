## What I Optimize For

- Shipping useful prototypes quickly without letting the codebase turn into a mess.
- Product thinking: workflows, data modeling, interaction details, and presentation polish.
- Local-first tools for sensitive business data.
- AI-assisted developer tooling and code quality systems.
- Interfaces that make complex planning, architecture, and operational workflows easier to reason about.

## Current Focus

I am focused on tools that help people move from fuzzy plans to working software: roadmap planners, architecture explorers, AI-code review systems, and focused product prototypes.

If you are reviewing my work, start with [Roadmap Studio](https://github.com/kcemate/roadmap-studio) and [Ratchet](https://github.com/kcemate/ratchet). They best show the mix of product judgment, implementation speed, and engineering pragmatism I care about.
